Just Before Slumber

just about now
I become able to speak well
in newborn fetal position
a ball
I call your name

as though rejoicing
in a happy thing
while knowing to start
is a terrifying thing

the light bulb's string
sways slightly when I stare above
in newborn fetal position
a ball
calling your name

am I here in this universe
I haven't even felt the world
yet my cells keep expanding

a voice, sucked in
the night I came to be alone
the night of everything
