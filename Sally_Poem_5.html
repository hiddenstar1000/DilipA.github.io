Scary Story

because time never moves at the same speed
there are terrifyingly fast mornings
and terrifyingly long nights

burnt an egg before it hatched
within the foul stench
a summer light
no goal for us
shrinking as we age
we cannot return

is the new
me beautiful?
you are beautiful
never moving at the same speed, so
next morning, the refrigerator is still cold
