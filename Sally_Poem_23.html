神妙に閉じた

だいぶ古びたタイルの床で

神妙に湿っていった

Door Was Made of Wood

on day when unimportant sounds ring out
door was made of wood
meekly creaked
and meekly closed

next to front door there is also bath room.
quite worn-out tiled floor
might accidentally slip, so
meekly got out of bath
and meekly dampened

our room is
small, but
filled with world
so overflowing that
kept moving on

someday      will arrive
different world will fill
and overflow

will forget
enough to forget the
newspaper delivery
decaying in one night
